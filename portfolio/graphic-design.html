Conference Booklet

I was able to make a typographic pamphlet centered around a talk that I really enjoyed from Elder Garret W. Gong. I learned a great deal about how to structure different pages and generally how to construct an easy to read pamphlet. Love is Spoken Here is a talk given by Elder Garret W. Gong of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles. It was given at the October 2023 session of the semi-annual General Conference of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I love this talk because it speaks about belonging and helping others to be cared for.
