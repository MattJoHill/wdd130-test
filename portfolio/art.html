Art

The Sun's Heat (Acryllic Painting)

This was a simple painting I made for a class project. It involved creating a composition that involved 6 flat dynamic shapes, and then overlaying different patterns on top of each one. I enjoyed the focal point I was able to create through the use of line and contrast here.

Self Portrait (Graphite)

It's me! I'm still not overly confident in my ability to draw, but I was relatively happy with how this turned out. My hair isn't exactly easy to draw as it's typically pretty messy, but I enjoyed the process of working through this and learning techniques that go into portraiture quite a bit!

Cutting Fruit (Graphite)

This was a still life drawing where I focused on shading. I started with a picture I took and examined the lighting and shadows throughout. It was extremely interesting to learn how light works on this small scale project. I also liked working with these organic shapes of the strawberry and banana that I had cut up. (Not to mention they were delicious after the camera was put away too!)
